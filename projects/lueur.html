Designing the Brand

Brainstorming for a brand was a critical part of the decision for starting as a product reseller/distributor of a company. The image that my brother and I adapted the concept from a morning glory flower. Unlike other resellers, we were seeking to innovate and to make our own brand and see how far we can go. In honor of Frontrow Enterprises, we got more customers and others even became referrals for the company.

Facts about the design:
-These two petals represent our products and supplements.
-The silhouette face of a lady and the hair represents our customers.
-The whole image represents the morning glory flower which correlates to our tagline "Reveal the new you." the same with the morning glory flower that glows when the sun rises.

I kept the design minimal, and straight to the point where it matches the product's purpose. Less is more, and we both liked the design, even we asked customers and received honest answers saying majority of them are liking it.
